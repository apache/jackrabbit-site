Jackrabbit Team

Apache Jackrabbit is the result of ongoing collaboration and contributions of a number of people. Everyone is welcome to contribute and all contributions are greatly appreciated.

The Jackrabbit project follows the meritocratic principles and role-based organization of the Apache Software Foundation.
