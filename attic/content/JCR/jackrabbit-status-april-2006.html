Jackrabbit Status April 2006

From the minutes of the Apache board meeting on April 26th, 2006:

Over the past month, Apache Jackrabbit completed its graduation from incubator, moved to its new home at jackrabbit.apache.org, and made our big 1.0 release using the mirrors.

  • http://jackrabbit.apache.org/
  • http://www.apache.org/dist/jackrabbit/RELEASE-NOTES.txt

The only thing of board interest is that we have implemented the file header proposed on legal-discuss last year.
